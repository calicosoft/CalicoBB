We use Gravatar to display avatars. Gravatar (Globally Recognized Avatar) allows you to use the same avatar on many sites, without having to upload each and every time.
To make your Gravatar appear on the forums, you must sign up for Gravatar with the same email address you used to sign up for the forums.
To change your Gravatar, login to your account (with the same email address you used on the forums) and change it from within.

Why is my Gravatar not appearing?
- please make sure your primary email address with Gravatar is the same email address you used to sign up for the forums
- reload your cache by deleting browsing history & cookies, and using SHIFT + F5
- check you have not disabled avatar viewing in your forum options
