Thank you for your payment!
Once your payment has been marked complete, you will receive a private message and will be able to download your file(s). Payment validation can take up to 48 hours (usually instantly).

Further information:
PayPal orders:
PayPal can take up to 48 hours to notify us of your payment. If you paid using your account balance or via your credit/debit card, we should recieve notification instantly. If you paid via an eCheque or direct debit, you will need to wait until the funds have been cleared.

Account Balance orders:
If you have sufficient funds in your account balance, your payment will be made instantly. Please check your private messages for your invoice & confirmation.

DOWNLOADING YOUR FILE(S):
To download your file, view your invoice and click "Download". Your order must be marked as "Paid" in order to download your file(s).
